Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

1. Introduction

GlucoTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, and use your health information.

2. Information We Collect

All data is stored locally on your device. We may collect the following:

  • Blood glucose readings
  • Insulin dosage records
  • Timestamps of entries
  • Symptoms and notes you provide

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your glucose history and trends
  • Generate statistics and summaries
  • Allow you to export your data

4. Data Storage & Security

Your health data is stored securely on your device. We do not:

  • Upload your data to servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell or rent your personal information
  • Collect analytics without your consent

5. Data Sharing

We never share your personal health data unless you explicitly export it or manually share it from your device.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Export: Download your data in CSV or JSON format
  • Delete: Remove all data by clearing app data in device settings

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Medical Disclaimer

GlucoTrack is a tracking tool and does not provide medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for guidance.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
